Animal Welfare Donation

Signs?

1. Pain when being touched, pet or lifted

2. Reluctance or difficulty when climbing stairs or jumping

3. Difficulty when getting up after lying down

4. Negative changes in attitude or behavior

Prerequisites?

1. Phone; phone numbers of local animal control, a shelter, and a 24-hour emergency veterinary clinic

2. Cat carrier or cardboard box

3. Collars and strong leashes for dogs

4. Heavy blanket; water bowls and water

5. Strong-smelling foods, such as canned tuna or dried liver

6. An animal first-aid kit.

How to approach?

Use caution when approaching the animal.

Should you succeed in getting close enough to capture them, you stand a good chance of being scratched or bitten.

When moving toward the animal, speak calmly to reassure them.

Make sure they can see you at all times as you approach.

Entice them to come to you by offering a strong-smelling food such as canned tuna or dried liver.
